# 🌊 VELA Protocol

> **Separating Yield from Risk, Unlocking Predictable On-Chain Returns**

VELA is a Solana-native structured financial protocol that revolutionizes DeFi by separating yield generation from price risk. We offer predictable, hedgeable, and risk-controllable returns that empower users to maximize their crypto assets with confidence.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🔒 **Flexible Staking System**

Lock your tokens and earn daily interest with three flexible periods:

- **7-Day Lock** – Quick returns for active traders
- **30-Day Lock** – Balanced yield for steady growth
- **90-Day Lock** – Maximum returns for long-term holders

Interest is calculated hourly and can be claimed anytime. Your principal is safely returned when the lock period ends.

### 💎 **Node NFT Program**

Boost your earnings with exclusive Node NFTs:

- **Diamond Node** (Limited to 600) – 20% interest boost
- **Gold Node** (Limited to 12000) – 10% interest boost

Node NFTs are tradeable and transferable. When you bind an NFT to your account, you unlock enhanced yields and access to exclusive node pool rewards.

### 🎁 **Node Pool Rewards**

Node holders share weekly reward pools:

- Diamond and Gold pools accumulate separately each week
- Previous week's pool becomes claimable when the new week starts
- Rewards are distributed proportionally among all node holders
- Claim your share every week – it's automatic and fair

### 🤝 **Community Referral System**

Build your network and earn together:

- **Direct Rewards** – Earn 5% from your direct referrals' staking interest
- **Team Rewards** – Earn from your entire network based on community level (L0-L7)
- **Multi-Level Structure** – Your community grows as your network expands
- **Transparent Tracking** – Monitor your team's performance and earnings in real-time

### 🔐 **Locked Token Vault**

A permanently locked token pool ensures protocol sustainability:

- Tokens locked into the vault can never be withdrawn
- Provides long-term stability and trust for the ecosystem
- Transparent and verifiable on-chain

### 📊 **Dynamic Rate Mechanism**

Smart economic design for sustainable growth:

- Interest rates adjust through halving mechanism based on total network output
- Daily deposit caps prevent system overload
- Rates are locked at order creation – your returns are predictable
